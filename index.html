Our Solutions

AUTONOMOUS

Edge AI-Driven Drone Operation with Object Detection

Our advanced autonomous drone surveillance system leverages Edge AI technology for real-time object detection, sectional identification, and target tracking. Operating seamlessly even in non-GPS environments, this system maintains continuous situational awareness while ensuring human oversight for critical decisions.

Key features include AI-powered object detection, intelligent obstacle avoidance, sectional identification of objects and vehicles, and secure, encrypted communications—optimized for reliable performance in complex and dynamic environments.

COUNTER-UAS

Drone Detection and Response Solutions

Our system uses advanced RF and Software-Defined Radio (SDR) technology to detect, track, and respond to unauthorized drones. It identifies drone signals across various frequency ranges, providing early alerts even in challenging environments.

Designed for flexibility, the solution can be deployed as a fixed installation or quickly set up in the field. It offers detailed spectrum analysis, automatic threat identification, and a range of response options, including signal disruption or safe intervention. This system is ideal for environments where proactive drone management is essential.

COMMUNICATIONS

LoRaWAN Communication Solution

Our LoRaWAN-based communication system enables drones to maintain reliable connectivity with ground units over extensive distances, even in non-GPS environments. This technology provides secure, low-power, long-range communication essential for operations in remote or challenging terrain.

With transmission ranges up to 10km in optimal conditions and mesh networking capabilities, our solution ensures continuous tactical communications in areas where traditional networks fail. The system supports encrypted data transfer, real-time telemetry, and command relay with minimal latency.
